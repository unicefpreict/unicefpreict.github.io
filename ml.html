Using The ARIMAS Model

ARIMA is an acronym that stands for AutoRegressive Integrated Moving Average. It is a class of model that captures a suite of different standard temporal structures in time series data. Since our dataset also looks at coverage over time, the ARIMAS model worked best for our purpose.
