Vaccination Data Project

A Rutgers Bootcamp Final Project Presentation.

About Our Project

Our project hopes to look at coverage percentages for vaccinations provided by UNICEF. Our prediction methods will try to calculate where certain regions of the world will be coverage-wise in the following years. Ideally, regions should be aiming to be for coverage in the 95% to 100% range. This according to the data being in this range of coverage means that 95% to 100% of all the children in the population are immunized against certain vaccines.

The dataset also provides insight into the individual vaccines. We can look at coverage percentages per region and per vaccine. We can deduce which vaccines need to be focused on to increase coverage for a specific region. Our goal is to be able to take this data and present it either to UNICEF or vaccine manufactures.
