Data, Code, And Software

Data Source

A joint collaboration between UNICEF and WHO, the dataset contains coverage percentages per vaccine per country from 1980 to 2018. There is also a target column that projects how children will be vaccinated that year. The vaccinated column is the actual ammount vaccinated at the end of the year. The data is also separated into workbooks, each representing a different vaccine. The final sheet contains global statistics. For a portion of our analysis, we used the global stats.
